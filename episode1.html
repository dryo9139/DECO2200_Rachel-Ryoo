ASSESSMENT 1 OVERVIEW

To briefly overview what we had to do for assessment 1, we had to gather our research both individually and as a group, researching which field we will be focusing on for building a futuristic (next 10 years) autonomous device. Our group chose to research the PARAMEDICS and EMERGENCY SERVICES field, as this field have many areas with potentials of improving and providing better services for both patients and paramedics.

1. BRAINSTORM & SKETCH

We sent out questionnaires, interviews and surveys with people who had experienced the paramedic services (patients or paramedics). The main highlights we have learnt from this was that TIME is important for the paramedics as they need to go to the patient AS FAST AS POSSIBLE. Paramedics are facing difficulties when there is heavy traffic and when cars don’t move out of the way. Also, The MACHINES and EQUIPMENTS used by the paramedics need to be updated with accuracy as it is not up-to-date with technology and the paramedics need to read it manually instead of a number showing up for easier view. The paramedics also finds it hard to use the stretcher in certain situations considering the environment (rocky roads, mountains, countryside, etc). We could also gain insights from the patient's point of view - that many patients feel ANXIOUS and ANNOYED when waiting for the ambulance, especially when they are coming particularly slow. Especially because the patients don’t know what to do with the injured area, the waiting time feels longer than usual. .
